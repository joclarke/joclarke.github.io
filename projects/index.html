This page serves as a record for various projects on which I've worked.

1. The Website Challenge

For this challenge, we were tasked with creating a website for a non-profit in Providence called Mixed Magic Theatre. MMT is a not-for-profit theatre company dedicated to putting diverse images on stage, giving a voice to the marginalized through theatre.

Below is a screenshot from the site we designed. Our Squarespace account had expired when we took the image, so our images no longer render on the page. We went a little too minimalistic with the site, but learned a great deal from the process with regard to good UX/UI design.

2. The Pecha Kucha

We were tasked with creating and presenting a pecha kucha-- a five minute presentation of 15 slides which advanced automatically every 20 seconds. I chose to discuss the importance of storytelling as my topic and hand-doodled my slides.

3. The Fellow Founder Challenge

In this challenge, we completed different tasks for two companies founded by older Fellows. We developed a six-month marketing strategy for the new e-commerce platform in the works for ColorHub's B2C corrugated cardboard printing service and executed a guerilla marketing campaign to sign up beta testers for a social media network called Yodel.
