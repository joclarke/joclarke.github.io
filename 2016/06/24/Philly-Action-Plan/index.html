Philly Action Plan

Jun 24, 2016

I didn’t see it coming…

But it happened! I’m committed to work at LUXTECH, LLC in Philadelphia, PA. As a grown and groomed Southern boy, this will be a significant shift from the Georgia, Tennessee, Virginia locales I’ve inhabited over the last 22.5 years. As the move looms, I want to start thinking about what I can do in my first 3 - 6 months to make the city my home and my home a better place.

Action Plan

Work

  1. I plan on going in as equipped as possible to succeed in Technical Sales. This means sucking the marrow out of training camp, and doing as much research as I can into LEDs and the industry on the side.

  2. I want to suss out a solid understanding of what a sustainable effort level will look like. I want to go in strong and stay strong– avoid burnout. This means figuring out what long-term healthy effort will be.

  3. Along with (2), I’d like to establish a healthy routine before I even leave camp– going to bed on time, waking up early to exercise, etc.

  4. Work on a side project related to work but not directly. Probably skills development, like UX/UI design.

City

  1. I want to be constantly investing in the communities that Philly will bring with it. This includes the other Fellows headed that way, finding a good church, a good jiu-jitsu gym, etc.

  2. I will look into volunteer opportunities as soon as possible. I’ve always wanted to be a volunteer firefighter.

  3. Find my coffee shop. Get a dog. Have someone over for dinner minimum once a week.
